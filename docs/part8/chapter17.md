# Chapter 17: 13-Week Learning Plan

## Introduction

This chapter provides a structured 13-week learning plan to master Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. Follow this schedule to learn systematically and build your capstone project.

## Overview

**Total Duration**: 13 weeks
**Weekly Commitment**: 10-15 hours
**Goal**: Complete understanding + Capstone project

## Week-by-Week Schedule

### Weeks 1-2: Introduction to Physical AI

#### Week 1: Foundations

**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand what Physical AI is
- Learn difference between digital and physical AI
- Explore humanoid robotics landscape
- Understand why simulation matters

**Activities:**
- Read Chapters 1-2
- Watch introductory videos
- Explore example robots online
- Set up development environment

**Deliverables:**
- Summary of Physical AI concepts
- Environment setup complete
- List of interesting humanoid robots

**Time**: 10-12 hours

#### Week 2: Sensor Systems and Basics

**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand robot sensors

**Deliverables:**

## Study Tips

### Daily Routine

**Recommended Schedule:**
- **Morning** (2 hours): Read and study theory
- **Afternoon** (2-3 hours): Hands-on practice
- **Evening** (1 hour): Review and notes

**Weekly Total**: 10-15 hours

### Effective Learning Strategies

**1. Active Learning**
- Don't just read—code along
- Experiment with examples
- Break things and fix them
- Ask questions

**2. Practice Regularly**
- Code every day
- Build small projects
- Test concepts
- Iterate and improve

**3. Take Notes**
- Write down key concepts
- Document your code
- Create cheat sheets
- Review regularly

**4. Join Community**
- Join ROS 2 forums
- Participate in discussions
- Ask for help
- Help others

**5. Build Projects**
- Start small
- Increase complexity
- Apply what you learn
- Show your work

## Adjusting the Schedule

### If You're Behind

**Don't panic!**
- Review what you missed
- Focus on key concepts
- Skip less critical topics
- Extend timeline if needed

### If You're Ahead

**Great!**
- Dive deeper into topics
- Build extra projects
- Help others learn
- Explore advanced topics

### Customizing for Your Needs

**Adjust based on:**
- Your background (more/less time on basics)
- Your goals (focus on relevant topics)
- Your schedule (spread over more weeks)
- Your interests (spend more time on favorite topics)
